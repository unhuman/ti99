# Adventire — Game 1 (INTRO KINGDOM) Walkthrough

You win by carrying the **chalice** into the **gold castle hall**. You can carry only one
object (touch to pick up, FIRE to drop), so the game is a series of ferry trips. Dragons
ignore walls and **pursue you from room to room** (~1.3s behind) — but a slain dragon stays
dead for the whole game, so killing all three early with the sword makes everything easier.

## The map (with each room's on-screen wall color)

```
                 [White Castle]--[N Meadow]--[Red Corridor]--[Blue Maze N]
                  (white walls;   (green)      (RED walls:     (light blue)
                   BRIDGE inside)     |          SWORD)             |
                                 [GOLD CASTLE]                      |
                                  (yellow; start/WIN)               |
                                      |                             |
              [S Meadow]-----------[Purple Cave]------[Blue Maze S]--[Black Castle]
              (LIGHT GREEN:         (purple; sealed     (dark blue:    (black walls;
               GOLD/YELLOW KEY       chamber with        WHITE KEY,     yellow dragon;
               lies here)            BLACK KEY;          red dragon)    hall -> dark-red
                                     green dragon)                      DUNGEON: CHALICE)
```

Note the two easy-to-confuse rooms: the **sword** is in the **red** corridor (east of the
green North Meadow); the **gold/yellow key** is in the **light-green South Meadow** — not
in any corridor.

Dragons: **red** (fast) guards the white key in Blue Maze S; **green** prowls the Purple
Cave; **yellow** holds the Black Castle grounds. The **bat** starts in Blue Maze N and
steals whatever it touches — including out of your hands. Chase it and touch your item to
snatch it back.

## Step by step

1. **Get the sword.** From the gold castle go SOUTH to the North Meadow, then EAST to the
   Red Corridor. Take the **sword**. It kills a dragon on contact, carried or lying — the
   kill zone is wherever the sword sits, so keep it between you and a charging dragon
   (re-grab it on the other side to switch hands).
2. **Slay the green dragon.** Corridor WEST back to the meadow, SOUTH to the South Meadow,
   EAST into the Purple Cave. Let the green dragon charge into the blade.
3. **Slay the red dragon and take the white key.** Cave EAST into Blue Maze S. The fast red
   dragon dies on your sword like any other. Drop the sword and pick up the **white key**
   (remember where you left the sword — the bat may relocate it).
4. **Open the white castle, take the bridge.** Route: maze WEST → cave WEST → South Meadow
   NORTH → North Meadow WEST to the White Castle. Touch the gate with the key (it opens for
   good), walk up into the archway. Inside: the **bridge**. Grab it **by a side rail**.
5. **Bridge into the sealed chamber.** Carry the bridge back EAST → SOUTH → EAST to the
   Purple Cave. The chamber is the walled box at the top right. Stand below its bottom
   wall, position the bridge over the wall, press FIRE — it snaps to the grid. Walk up
   through the dark channel and take the **black key**. Walk back out the channel.
6. **Slay the yellow dragon and open the black castle.** If you want it dead first, fetch
   the sword; otherwise dodge. Route: cave EAST → Blue Maze S EAST to the Black Castle.
   Open the gate with the black key, enter the hall, go EAST into the Dungeon: the
   shimmering **chalice**.
7. **Open the gold castle.** The **gold/yellow key** lies in the **light-green South
   Meadow** (unguarded). Carry it to the gold castle gate and open it. (Do this before
   hauling the chalice, so the last trip is nonstop.)
8. **Bring the chalice home.** Carry the chalice: Dungeon WEST → hall SOUTH → Black Castle
   grounds WEST → Blue Maze S WEST → Cave WEST → South Meadow NORTH → Meadow NORTH → through
   the open gold gate into the hall. Color flash — the chalice is home.

## If things go wrong

- **The bat stole your key/sword:** follow it and touch the object to take it back.
- **Swallowed by a dragon:** FIRE restarts a fresh game from the title.
- **An object seems unreachable:** in Game 1 there is no magnet — but nothing here can
  land anywhere the bridge can't reach.
