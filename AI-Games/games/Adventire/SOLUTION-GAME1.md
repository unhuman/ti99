# Adventire — Game 1 (INTRO KINGDOM) Walkthrough

You win by carrying the **chalice** into the **gold castle hall**. You can carry only one
object (touch to pick up, FIRE to drop), so the game is a series of ferry trips. Dragons
ignore walls and **pursue you from room to room** (~1.3s behind) — but a slain dragon stays
dead for the whole game, so killing all three early with the sword makes everything easier.

## The map (with each room's on-screen wall color)

```
                 [White Castle]--[N Meadow]--[Red Corridor]--[Blue Maze N]
                  (white walls;   (green)      (RED walls:     (light blue)
                   BRIDGE inside)     |          SWORD)             |
                                 [GOLD CASTLE]                      |
                                  (yellow; start/WIN)               |
                                      |                             |
              [S Meadow]-----------[Purple Cave]------[Blue Maze S]--[Black Castle]
              (LIGHT GREEN:         (purple; sealed     (dark blue:    (black walls;
               GOLD/YELLOW KEY       chamber with        WHITE KEY,     yellow dragon;
               lies here)            BLACK KEY;          red dragon)    hall -> dark-red
                                     green dragon)                      DUNGEON: CHALICE)
```

Note the two easy-to-confuse rooms: the **sword** is in the **red** corridor (east of the
green North Meadow); the **gold/yellow key** is in the **light-green South Meadow** — not
in any corridor.

Dragons: **red** (fast) guards the white key in Blue Maze S; **green** prowls the Purple
Cave; **yellow** holds the Black Castle grounds. The **bat** starts in Blue Maze N and
steals whatever it touches — including out of your hands. Chase it and touch your item to
snatch it back.

## Step by step (each move = one screen)

Start: standing below the yellow gold castle.

1. **Get the sword.**
   DOWN 1 (green meadow) → RIGHT 1 (**red** corridor). Take the **sword**. It kills a
   dragon on contact, carried or lying — the kill zone is wherever the sword sits, so keep
   it between you and a charging dragon (re-grab it on the other side to switch hands).
2. **Slay all three dragons** (they stay dead all game). Sword in hand:
   LEFT 1 (green meadow) → DOWN 1 (light-green meadow) → RIGHT 1 (purple cave): the
   **green dragon** dies on the blade. RIGHT 1 (dark-blue maze): the fast **red dragon**
   dies. RIGHT 1 (black castle): the **yellow dragon** dies. Drop the sword here.
3. **White key.** LEFT 1 (dark-blue maze): take the **white key** (it was the red dragon's
   treasure).
4. **White castle → bridge.**
   LEFT 1 (purple cave) → LEFT 1 (light-green meadow) → UP 1 (green meadow) → LEFT 1
   (white castle). Touch the gate with the key — it opens for good. Walk UP into the
   archway (inside). Touch the **bridge** to take it (the white key drops here; you're
   done with it). Grab the bridge **by a side rail**.
5. **Bridge into the sealed chamber → black key.**
   Walk DOWN through the hall's bottom doorway (outside) → RIGHT 1 (green meadow) →
   DOWN 1 (light-green meadow) → RIGHT 1 (purple cave). The chamber is the walled box at
   the TOP RIGHT. Stand below its bottom wall, position the bridge over that wall, press
   FIRE — it snaps into place. Walk UP through the dark channel, touch the **black key**
   (swap), walk back DOWN out of the channel.
6. **Black castle → chalice.**
   RIGHT 1 (dark-blue maze) → RIGHT 1 (black castle). Touch the gate with the black key,
   walk UP into the archway (hall) → RIGHT 1 (dark-red dungeon): take the shimmering
   **chalice** (the black key drops here; done with it).
7. **Park the chalice, open the gold castle.**
   Carrying the chalice: LEFT 1 (hall) → DOWN through the bottom doorway (black castle
   grounds) → LEFT 1 (dark-blue maze) → LEFT 1 (purple cave) → LEFT 1 (light-green
   meadow). DROP the chalice here and take the **gold/yellow key** lying in this meadow.
   UP 1 (green meadow) → UP 1 (gold castle): touch the gate — it opens. Drop the key.
8. **Bring the chalice home.**
   DOWN 1 → DOWN 1 (light-green meadow): take the **chalice**. UP 1 → UP 1 (gold castle) →
   walk UP through the open gate into the hall. Color flash — the chalice is home.

## If things go wrong

- **The bat stole your key/sword:** follow it and touch the object to take it back.
- **Swallowed by a dragon:** FIRE restarts a fresh game from the title.
- **An object seems unreachable:** in Game 1 there is no magnet — but nothing here can
  land anywhere the bridge can't reach.
